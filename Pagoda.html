What is NEAR?

Certified Carbon Neutral

Build something great, while offsetting your CO2 footprint. NEAR is 100% carbon neutral and 200k times more energy efficient than Bitcoin.

Designed to Scale

NEAR is a proof-of-stake blockchain that ensures scalability through sharding. (Translation: you can scale fast, without all the growing pains.)

Low cost. High performance.

NEAR keeps performance high and fees low—no matter how much your startup grows.

Plays well with others

Effortlessly bridge your dapps to assets on Ethereum, Polkadot, Cosmos, Cardano, Solana, Terra, and more.
